I’ve so little words allowed to say
There’s no time to make up my failures

I’ve so many words desired to say
There’s no need to pick up my memories
And there’s no one to receive my heart

It’s so anxious to miss your figure
I’ve so much words never to say
There’s no place to bury my pieces
And there’s no magic to rewrite my code

It’s so stupid to keep silence
And there’s none to be continued
Any more!
